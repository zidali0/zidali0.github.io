Education

  • University of Michigan (UM)                                                                           Ann Arbor, MI
    Ph.D., Mechanical, Biomedical Engineering                   Aug. 2013 – Apr. 2018
    Advisor: Prof. Jianping Fu

    Read More

    I have worked on research projects in coagulation device design, microfluidics, neural differentiation of human pluripotent stem cells, and cancer metastasis. For details, please see Research page.
    I invested most of my graduate tuition credits on learning biology, including molecular and cellular biology, human physiology, cellular engineering, and cancer biology. I also learnt a lot of other biology by reading.

  • University of Hong Kong (HKU)                                                                          Hong Kong
    Research Assistant, mechanical engineering                              July 2012 – June 2013
    Advisor: Prof. Anderson Ho Cheung Shum   

    Read More

    This employment was in my gap year before Ph.D. study. My main project was to study the interface of aqueous two phase system (ATPS). I developed a platform to visualize the fluctuation in flow rate that syringe pumps supply. My colleague, Sibyl Mak, further developed a method to visualize music based on this platform, dubbed musical microfluidics. Details are on Research page.

  • Tsinghua University (THU)                                                                               Beijing, China
    Exchange Student, Aerospace Engineering                                            Sept. 2010 – Jan. 2011

  • University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)                      Hefei, Anhui, China
    B.Eng., Mechanical Engineering, GPA: 3.84/4.3                                   Aug. 2008 – June 2012

    Read More

    My study was focused on thermal science and energy engineering.
    My senior project was to develop a chemical pump based on membrane distillation.
