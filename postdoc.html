Postdoc openings in Micro-Bioengineering at Shenzhen University

Chinese version see below

Two to three postdoctoral positions are available in Biomedical Engineering at Shenzhen University, China.

Advisor:

Dr. Zida Li is an assistant professor in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Shenzhen University. He received his PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor while working with Dr. Jianping Fu. Before that, he worked as Research Assistant in Dr. Anderson Shum’s group at the University of Hong Kong. He is currently supported by the Shenzhen Oversea Talents Program.

Research:

Our research is on the nexus of micro/nanofabrication, interfacial science, and bioengineering, with a focus on microfluidics, mechanobiology, and single cell analysis. The goal of our lab is to solve fundamental and clinical problems by leveraging our engineering expertise. The ongoing projects include the study of point-of-care coagulation testing, mechanobiology of immune cells, and droplet-based single cell RNA sequencing. We collaborate closely with colleagues from other institutes, such as the University of Michigan, New York University, and Tsinghua University. Our lab has received support from a few grants, including a major government-funded start-up grant. For more information, please visit here.

Qualifications:

  • PhD with a strong background in:
    • Engineering: microfluidics, 3D printing, microfabrication, and functional materials
    • Biomedical: biochips, organoid, biomaterials, and tissue engineering.
  • Capability of working independently and thinking critically.
  • Research integrity.

Stipend and benefits:

  • Annual salary no less than 320k RMB (~45k USD)
  • Shenzhen University full-time staff benefits
  • Additional stipend from Shenzhen gov. (if applicable)

Application:

Applicants should send their curriculum vitae, names/addresses of at least two references, and cover letter by email (zidali AT szu.edu.cn) to Dr. Zida Li.

深圳大学微纳生物工程课题组招聘博士后

一、 导师介绍

李自达博士，深圳大学生物医学工程学院助理教授，深圳市“海外高层次人才C类”获得者。2012年于香港大学机械工程系Anderson Shum教授课题组担任研究助理，2018年于美国密歇根大学Jianping Fu教授课题组获得博士学位，2018年6月全职加入深圳大学。

二、课题组介绍

课题组主要研究方向包括：微流体、力学生物学、单细胞分析等。研究方向涉及微纳米加工、界面科学、生物工程等学科。加入深大以后，课题组已有研究成果发表在Biomicrofluidics, ACS Sensors, Advanced Energy Materials等杂志上；前期成果曾发表在Small, Lab on a Chip, Nature等杂志上。课题组与University of Michigan, NYU, HKU, 清华大学等境内外高校保持良好的科学与人才交流合作。课题组研究获深圳市“高层次人才科研启动”、广东省自然科学基金、广东省医学科研基金支持，经费充足。详情请访问课题组主页：https://zidalab.github.io/

三、招聘方向

拟在以下方向及交叉领域招聘博士后2-3名。

  • 工程科学背景：微流控技术、3D打印、微纳米制造
  • 生物医学背景：生物芯片、类器官、生物材料、组织工程

四、应聘条件

已获得博士学位或即将博士毕业的应届生，年龄一般不超过32岁，身体健康，近五年内在相关领域权威期刊发表过第一作者研究论文至少1篇。对科学研究充满热情，具备主动思考和独立解决问题的能力，工作认真，求实求是，有责任心，有团队协作能力。

五、薪酬待遇

将提供不低于深圳大学博士后的待遇，包括30+万的年薪（深圳市政府补贴18万/年、深圳大学补贴14万/年），并享受科研奖励，具体条件面议。进站时，可自愿选择落户深圳市。选择落户深圳市的，其配偶及未成年子女可办理随迁入户。子女入托、入学等按深圳市相关条例执行。支持申请深圳市和深圳大学的各类人才计划。博士后在站期间可以负责人身份申请各级科研课题资助。出站时，可享受深圳市对出站博士后给予的30万元资助，用于科研投入或创业前期费用。符合深圳大学政策要求的优秀博士后可申请转为教师。

六、联系方式

请将求职信、个人简历、推荐人信息（至少2位）等材料发送至：李自达（zidali AT szu.edu.cn），邮件主题“姓名-博士后申请”。本招聘信息长期有效，招满为止。
