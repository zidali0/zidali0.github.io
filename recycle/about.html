    Student Research Assistant, mechanical engineering                              July 2012 – June 2013
    Advisor: Prof. Anderson Ho Cheung Shum   

    Read More

    This was in my gap year before Ph.D. study. My main project was to study the interface of aqueous two phase system (ATPS). I developed a platform to visualize the fluctuation in flow rate that syringe pumps supply. My colleague, Sibyl Mak, further developed a method to visualize music based on this platform, dubbed musical microfluidics. Details are on Research page.
